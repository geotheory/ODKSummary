Data protection

The MSF data sharing policy is based on the principles of medical confidentiality, privacy, protection of sensitive data, equality among data users, promotion of health benefits to the greater population, sharing for the local benefit, intellectual property, collaborative partnership, and efficiency. This policy applies to all health data generated in MSF programs or sites, where MSF acts as a Custodian for such data. Please refer to the MSF Data Sharing Policy for more detailed information.
